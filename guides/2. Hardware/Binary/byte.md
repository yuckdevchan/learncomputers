# Byte
A [byte](byte) is a **unit** of data that is **8 bits** long. A **bit** is a **1** or a **0** (See [binary](binary)). 

(w) In **almost every computer** a byte is **8 bits long**, but it **doesn't have** to be. The word **byte** is **commonly used** to mean **8 bits long**, though. A more **correct term** would be **octet**.<br><br>This **guide** will use **the word byte** to mean **8 bits long**.
