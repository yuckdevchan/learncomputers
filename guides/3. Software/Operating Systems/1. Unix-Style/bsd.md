# BSD
[BSD](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berkeley_Software_Distribution) is a family of [unix-style](whats-unix) [operating systems](whats-an-operating-system).

Like [Linux](linux), many modern day servers and consumer devices use BSD without you knowing.

Many companies decide to use a BSD distribution instead of a [Linux](linux) distribution because of more permissive licensing.
