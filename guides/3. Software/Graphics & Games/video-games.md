# Video Games
[Video Games](video-games) are a type of [software application](application) that are designed to entertain the user.

Whether video games are 2D or 3D, they (with almost no exceptions) utilise a [graphics library](graphics-libraries-and-apis) to render their graphics on the screen. Some use a full [game engine](game-engine).

Video games with realistic graphics or complex mechanics push the boundaries of what their generation of hardware can handle. They often require powerful [GPUs](gpu) and [CPUs](cpu) and plenty of [RAM](ram) to run at a consistent [frame rate](frame-rate) and a high [display resolution](resolution).

## Licensing
Almost all video games are [proprietary software](proprietary-software).
